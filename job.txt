The Role

Scotiabank is looking for a motivated and enthusiastic Junior Full Stack Developer (Level 6) to join our growing engineering team. We are seeking someone who is passionate about technology, eager to learn, and excited to contribute across the full software development lifecycle.

This role is ideal for a developer with hands-on experience in JavaScript and ReactJS, who thrives in a collaborative environment and demonstrates initiative, accountability, and a strong work ethic. You will work closely with developers, designers, product owners, and backend teams to build scalable and modern banking applications.

    BaaS Layer: Java
